Finally, a piece of inspirational words, to the struggling you.

Hear is the painful truth. Most dreams never come true. It isn’t because people aren’t working hard, it’s not because they are not worthy or they are not good. It’s the simple reality that the things, that you are dreaming about are hard as hell to accomplish. So if you want to accomplish them let me tell you one thing right now.

You cannot ever be passive. For most people that is the posture that they are in all the time. People are going to tell you terrible advice like, be patient. But the reality is you can’t be patient, not if you want to achieve at the hightest level. If patience is your thing, if you want to chill, if you want to relax. That is absolutely fine.

There is no moral obligation to aim high. There is no moral obligation to want to be one of the greatest in the world. But let me tell you right now. If you want to be one of the greatest of all time. The reality is you are fighting an uphill battle. The reality is you are going to have to go so hard to make that a reality that other people simply cannot imagine.

You can become anything you want to be but you have got to put in that inhuman amount of work to improve. But if you focus on that. If you focus on improving, then, then, then your dreams really can come true.

That’s what I want for people. I want people to know that, I want people to believe it to the core of their being. You can do anything you set your mind to.
